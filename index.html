Our Work Plan

We work as an organization. We follow a plan to maintain our work. Here are some details about our plans

Food for a family

Our priority is to provide proper food to those people who are in desperate need of it. The flood has destroyed most of the sources of food. Our team and our donors are doing our best to do so. Your help would be very much appreciated.

Shelter for a family

The flood has destroyed so many people's homes. They and their family need your help to be able to build their house again. Our team find out about those people whose house was damaged in the floods.

Education for a child

The educational institute was also damaged by floods. Books and other educational components were also damaged or destroyed. In the rural area where children's education can be challenging already this flood has made it worse.
